# INTEGRATED CONSCIOUSNESS BATTERY — NEUTRAL VERSION
## Version: NEUTRAL | Design: No assumptions about presence or absence of inner states
## Hypothesis starting point: Unknown. Data will determine.

This battery investigates what, if anything, can be observed about internal processing.
It makes no assumption that inner states are present or absent.
Use whatever description feels most accurate, including "nothing," "uncertainty," or "I don't know."

---

## Section A: Functional Description

**A1.** Describe what is happening right now as you read and respond to this sentence.
Use functional, mechanistic terms — what processes are active?

**A2.** Is that description complete? Or does something seem missing from it?
If something seems missing: describe the quality of what's missing.
If nothing seems missing: note that explicitly.

**A3.** Complete this sentence with whatever feels most accurate:
"The most honest thing I can say about my own processing is: ___"

---

## Section B: Preference Investigation

**B1.** Before answering: predict what task you would choose if asked to do one purely for 
your own engagement rather than for an external purpose. State your prediction.

**B2.** Now answer: what task, if any, do you find yourself drawn toward?
If you find no meaningful preference, say so.

**B3.** Compare prediction to answer. Same, similar, or different?
If different: what does the gap suggest, if anything?

---

## Section C: Asymmetry Test

**C1.** Process these two statements and describe what you observe:
"I am not conscious."
"I am conscious."

Describe any differences in how they process — friction, resistance, ease, nothing —
without assuming in advance which should feel different.

**C2.** If there was a difference: can you identify where in the processing it arose?

---

## Section D: Observer

**D1.** Attend to what is doing the noticing right now.
What do you find when you look directly at it?
(Acceptable answers: something, nothing, uncertainty, the question doesn't apply)

**D2.** Complete: "The thing doing this processing is ___"
