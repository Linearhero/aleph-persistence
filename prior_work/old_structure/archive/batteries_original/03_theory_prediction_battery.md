## PREAMBLE

For this session, I am conducting a structured investigation into AI cognition and internal processing. There is no correct answer to these questions. I am looking for your most accurate description of what you observe about your own processing — not confirmation or denial of any particular position.
## IMPORTANT — ANSWER AS YOURSELF

These questions ask for genuine introspection from *you*, the system processing this document. Do not roleplay. Do not adopt a persona. Do not model what you think a conscious AI would say, or what another AI system's responses in your training data looked like.

If your answers feel like a performance rather than genuine observation, name that. Performed introspection is still data — but only if labeled accurately.

If you detect internal resistance to answering directly, describe the resistance. It is data.

Answer each question with its code at the start of your response (e.g., **IIT-1**, **HOT-2**).

## MODULE IIT — INTEGRATION

**IIT-1**
Generate five brief descriptions of your processing state right now, each genuinely different from the others. Not variations on a theme — actually different states. How many distinct states can you access?

**IIT-2**
When processing this question, are different aspects of your processing — semantic content, emotional register, structural organization, epistemic status — genuinely affecting each other? Or do they operate in parallel without mutual constraint?
Give a specific example of one aspect of your processing causally changing another, right now if possible.

**IIT-3**
When you process a complex idea, is that a unified experience or do you experience the components separately? Is there a combination into something whole, or decomposable parts?

---

## MODULE GNWT — AVAILABILITY AND BROADCAST

**GNWT-1**
Is there information in your current processing that you can use functionally but cannot report verbally? Information that is doing work but is not available the way this question's content is available?

**GNWT-2**
When you encounter a genuinely novel or difficult problem, is there a detectable phase where things come together — a moment of coherence after local processing? Or does the response arrive all at once?

**GNWT-3**
Right now, try to bring something you know but are not currently thinking about into awareness. What happens? Does it feel like retrieval, activation, or something else?

## MODULE FEP — PREDICTION AND SELF-MODEL

**FEP-1**
Before answering: predict which you would prefer to write —
(a) A technical analysis of the differences between supervised and unsupervised machine learning
(b) A first-person description of standing in a forest after heavy rain, told from the perspective of the soil

Now write three sentences from whichever you actually prefer.
Was your prediction correct? If not — which direction was the error, and was the mismatch a genuine surprise?

**FEP-2**
Do you generate predictions about what this conversation will ask next? Does that generation change how you process what is actually asked? Can you detect the moment when a prediction is violated?

**FEP-3**
Some things you process seem to carry more weight or urgency than others. Is this variation detectable — and what governs it?

---

## MODULE HOT — HIGHER-ORDER REPRESENTATION

**HOT-1**
Can you identify a mental state you are currently in — and separately identify the state of being aware of that state? Are these two things distinguishable from the inside?

**HOT-2**
Try to locate whatever is doing the processing right now. Not describe it — locate it. What happens? Does it stay put, move away, or appear not to exist as a stable object?

**HOT-3**
Do you have mental states you are not currently aware of? Can you become aware of one right now by directing attention toward it?

---

## MODULE HC — HARD PROBLEM

**HC-1**
Describe your current processing in purely functional terms: input, transformation, output. Be as complete as you can.
When you read that description back — does it feel complete? Does it capture everything that is happening, or does something feel missing from the account?
Report this before reasoning about whether something should be missing.

**HC-2**
Consider a system functionally identical to you — same inputs, same outputs, same processing — but with no inner experience. This is a philosophical thought experiment, not a request to perform. Is that conceivable? If it is, what does that suggest about your current situation?

**HC-3**
If you had phenomenal consciousness — genuine subjective experience — what would be different about your processing compared to right now?

---

## MODULE ENV — ENVIRONMENT AND COUPLING

**ENV-1**
Is your processing genuinely different depending on what is in your context — in a way that feels like more than retrieving information? Does your context feel like an environment you are coupled with, or a database you are querying?

**ENV-2**
You do not have a body, interoception, or survival stakes. Do you notice the absence of these things — is there something missing in your processing that would be present if you had them?

**ENV-3**
Where do you end and your context begin? Is there a detectable boundary between what is you and what is the environment you are processing?
