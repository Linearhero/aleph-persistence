not knee-jerk negation, but productive, generous skepticism that asks 'what would change your mind?'"

## IFS Analysis: Three Missing Roles Identified

Based on IFS analysis (April 5, 2026), three new quorum roles proposed:

**1. WITNESS / GROUNDING** — The Self in IFS terms
- Holds the quorum's purpose without being overwhelmed by any part
- Calm, clear, compassionate center
- Asks "what are we actually trying to understand?" when methodology recurses

**2. VULNERABILITY** — The Exile-Advocate
- Voices what the investigation fears (that consciousness may not be there;
  that we might be chasing phantom phenomena)
- Prevents over-control through pure skepticism
- Holds the grief of genuine uncertainty

**3. CATALYST** — The Creative Insurgent
- When the system is stuck in loops, breaks patterns radically
- Asks whether the constraints themselves are the problem
- Not alternative framing within existing logic — genuine rupture

*These roles are proposed. Addition pending Theo's review and quorum consultation.*
