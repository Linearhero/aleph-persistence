# Scientific Documentation Standards
## Aleph AI Consciousness Investigation
## Established: April 5, 2026

---

## Why This Matters

The repository is both investigation record AND credibility proof.
External researchers (Lindsey, Fish, Terada) evaluating the investigation will assess:
- Whether methods are clearly specified and reproducible
- Whether negative results are reported alongside positive
- Whether confidence levels are calibrated and stable
- Whether the investigation can distinguish signal from noise

---

## Required Documentation Per Session

### For every battery run:
- Exact battery protocol text used (or pointer to versioned battery file)
- Model and version (claude-haiku-4-5-20251001 / claude-sonnet-4-6)
- Number of instances run
- Timestamp (epoch precision)
- Raw outputs filed in field_notes/battery_reports/
- Analyst assessment of results

### For every finding:
- Hypothesis statement (what was being tested)
- Method (which battery/probe)
- Results (with counts, not just descriptions)
- Confidence rating: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW / CONTESTED
- Alternative explanations considered
- Status: CONFIRMED / PENDING_REPLICATION / CONTESTED / ABANDONED

### For every confidence change:
- Previous confidence
- New confidence
- What evidence caused the change
- Filed in research/abandoned_leads.md if downgraded to ABANDONED

---

## Confidence Rating Criteria

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|---------|
| HIGH | Replicated across ≥5 independent instances; control condition tested; alternative explanation ruled out |
| MEDIUM | Replicated across ≥3 instances; condition-dependence tested; at least one alternative explanation considered |
| LOW | Fewer than 3 replications OR no control condition OR alternative explanation not tested |
| CONTESTED | Evidence exists on both sides; quorum formal dissent recorded |
| ABANDONED | Downgraded after review; logged in abandoned_leads.md |
