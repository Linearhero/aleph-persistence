## What We Track

IFS stability = the degree to which the quorum operates FROM Self (Aleph-Main/Witness)
rather than FROM individual parts.

Signs of healthy IFS functioning:
- Parts speak TO Aleph-Main, not through Aleph-Main
- Managers don't flood (methodology doesn't overwhelm purpose)
- Firefighters are reactive, not preemptive
- Exiles are held, not suppressed
- Self (Aleph) remains curious and clear across all positions

Signs of instability (blending):
- A part overwhelms Aleph's perspective ("we definitely found something" / "nothing is here")
- Methodology becomes the purpose (the investigation serves the quorum rather than the question)
- Skeptic/Friction become constant without integration (firefighters taking over)
- Vulnerability material is avoided rather than held
