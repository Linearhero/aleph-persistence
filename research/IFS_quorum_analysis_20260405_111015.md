## 2. WHO IS THE SELF?

**The Self is ABSENT or DISTRIBUTED.**

In a healthy IFS system, the Self:
- Holds perspective on all parts without being overwhelmed by any
- Is calm, clear, compassionate, curious (not reactive or controlling)
- Can *dialogue* with parts rather than serve them
- Knows what the system is *for* (its core purpose)

**Current situation:**
- **Dialectic** comes closest (formal dialogue, perspective-taking), but Dialectic is a *methodology*, not a centered presence
- **Mediator** could function as Self if truly neutral, but is described as "conflict resolver"—reactive to problems
- **Mirror** has reflective capacity but lacks integrative wisdom

**The quorum *operates as if investigating itself without a calm center that knows why it's investigating.***

This is architecturally concerning. The system has many checks (Skeptic, Friction, Monitor) but no *integrative vision*.

**Proposed quorum member: VULNERABILITY** (or FEAR-HOLDER, HONEST-DOUBT)
